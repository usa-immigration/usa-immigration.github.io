How people enter the United States illegally

According to a studying conducted by the Department of Homeland Security, over 500,000 people illegally overstayed their tourist or business visas, in 2015. It was estimated that in 2016, 100,000 more visas would be overstayed. These numbers are expected get progressively higher.

The United States grants asylum to indivduals who are fleeing persecution due to their race, religion, nationality, or political opinion. In 2015, the United States granted asylum to over 26,000 immigrants. This is a small fraction of the number of refugees who apply for asylum.
