Immigration Policies

Family Based

Immediate relatives or spouses are admitted as US citizens if their family member is a citizen Every year the number of visas given varies. The lowest number the United States is permitted to give is 226,000, but realistically there are over 480,000 given. Family members are giving preferences (children, parents, and spouses)

Employment Based

Immigrants that have valuable skills are given visas Temporary and permanent visas given More than 20 types temporary visas 140,000 permanent visas are given a year. This number includes the workers and their families. For permanent visas, there are 5 preference categories. Depending on how valuable the United States deems your ability, the higher preference you are.
